Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Inventos (“we”, “our”, “us”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our applications, websites, and related services.

1. Overview

Inventos provides IT asset management and analytics software for enterprises. By accessing or using our services, you acknowledge that you have read this Privacy Policy and consent to the handling of your information as described here.

2. Definitions

“Services”
Any hosted application, API, website, or related feature offered by Inventos.
“Personal Data”
Any information that identifies or can be reasonably linked to an identifiable person, including account details, contact information, or device identifiers.
“Processing”
Any operation performed on data, including collection, storage, usage, sharing, or deletion.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Information You Provide

We collect Personal Data that you submit directly, including:

  • Account registration details such as name, email, company, and role.
  • Billing information including payment method and billing contact.
  • Support tickets, survey responses, and other communication with our teams.

3.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you access or use our Services, we automatically collect:

  • Log data (IP address, browser type, operating system, timestamps).
  • Usage analytics to understand features utilized and performance.
  • Device information such as hardware identifiers and settings.

3.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive Personal Data from trusted partners, such as identity providers, integration partners, and public databases, to support authentication and compliance.

4. How We Use Your Information

Your information enables us to:

  1. Deliver, maintain, and improve the Services.
  2. Authenticate users, manage access, and provide customer support.
  3. Process transactions, subscriptions, and purchase requests.
  4. Send security alerts, feature updates, and administrative messages.
  5. Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or security incidents.
  6. Comply with legal obligations and enforce our agreements.

5. Legal Bases for Processing

Depending on your jurisdiction, we rely on the following legal bases:

  • Contractual necessity: Processing to provide the Services you requested.
  • Legitimate interests: Improving service quality, ensuring security, and growing our business.
  • Consent: For marketing emails or optional data collection where required.
  • Legal obligation: Compliance with applicable laws and regulations.

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell Personal Data. We may disclose information with:

  • Service providers assisting with hosting, analytics, billing, and customer support.
  • Integration partners when you explicitly enable or configure such connections.
  • Law enforcement or regulators if required by law or to protect rights and safety.
  • Related entities in the context of a merger, acquisition, or financing event.

7. Data Retention

We retain Personal Data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. When data is no longer required, we securely delete or anonymize it.

8. International Transfers

Inventos operates globally. When Personal Data is transferred across borders, we implement appropriate safeguards, such as Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent mechanisms, to ensure adequate protection under applicable laws.

9. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access and obtain a copy of your Personal Data.
  • Rectify inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Request deletion or restriction of your data.
  • Object to processing or withdraw consent where applicable.
  • Receive your data in a portable format.

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@inventos.io. We will respond within the timeframes required by law.

10. Security

We apply administrative, technical, and physical controls to protect Personal Data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. Measures include encrypted data storage, access controls, monitoring, and employee training. No system is entirely secure, therefore you share information at your own risk.

11. Children’s Privacy

Our Services are not directed to individuals under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect Personal Data from children. If we become aware that a child has provided us with information, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

12. Third-Party Services

Our Services may include links or integrations with third-party products. This Privacy Policy does not cover those services. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services you interact with.

13. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes to our practices or legal requirements. We will post the updated policy on this page and, when appropriate, notify you via email or in-product message. The “Last Updated” date below indicates the latest revision.
